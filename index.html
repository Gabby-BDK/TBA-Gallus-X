Gallus X AI-Robotic Feeder with Rice Hull Management for Chickens

About

Gallus X is an AI-driven poultry management system designed to optimize feeding and maintain a healthier environment for chickens. Equipped with a smart camera, the system monitors and analyzes chicken behavioral patterns in real-time to determine the optimal feeding schedule, ensuring efficient resource use and healthier growth. The System also includes temperature and humidity sensors for continuous environmental monitoring, helping maintain ideal living conditions. Additionally, it features an ammonia sensor connected to a rice hull management system that helps neutralize odor and reduce harmful ammonia buildup, creating a cleaner and safer poultry environment.

Objectives

Specific Objective

To develop an IoT-Based System with an AI-Driven Feeder and Rice Hull Dispenser for chickens.

General Objectives

The general objectives of the system are as follows: to train a dataset using the YOLOv8 model for object detection, and to build a system that monitors temperature, humidity, and ammonia levels for chickens; automates rice hull dispensing to reduce ammonia concentration; uses AI-based image processing to automate chicken feeding; sends sensor data to ThingSpeak for real-time monitoring; and triggers email notifications for alert conditions. Additionally, it aims to determine the growth of chickens through Feed Conversion Ratio (FCR) analysis and to evaluate the overall functionality and reliability of the system.
